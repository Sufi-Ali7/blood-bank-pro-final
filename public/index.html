Privacy Policy

We store user accounts, blood inventory, requests, notifications, and donation history only for blood donation workflow, admin review, and emergency coordination. Sensitive authentication data is hashed. Demo email/SMS services remain configurable via environment variables.

Terms of Service

This application is intended for education, workflow simulation, and managed blood request coordination. Users must provide accurate blood group, city, and contact details.

Cookie Policy

The frontend stores auth session data locally in the browser to keep users logged in and personalize dashboards.

HIPAA Compliance

This project follows secure-by-default patterns for role access, hashed passwords, and controlled data display. Full statutory compliance still depends on production hosting, audit trails, and regulated deployment policies.
